Terramonitor goes WebWorldWind

Terramonitor is the most up-to-date cloudless satellite map of the globe and gives professionals the power to analyze, build and organize geographical information into actionable insights. The service provides global land coverage, daily updates, multiple data layers with timestamp and GIS integration. The images are produced by combining different satellite data sources up to 0.5 meter resolution. More info on the service can be found here: www.terramonitor.com

This application shows the Terramonitor True Color layer and the corresponding timestamp layer in the WebWorldWind Interface. It is a great example on how the Terramonitor data can be used for visualization, education and research.
